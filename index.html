The Music of

The Disco Biscuits

  • The Disco Biscuits are a jam band from Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • The band consists of Allen Aucoin (drums), Marc Brownstein (bass), Jon Gutwillig (guitar), and Aron Magner (keyboards, synths)
  • The band incorporates elements from a variety of musical genres into a consistent base of electronic and rock.
  • Their style has been described as trance fusion.
